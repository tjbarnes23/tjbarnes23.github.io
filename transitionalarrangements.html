2.

Any Peals as defined under this document that were previously rung but which were not recognized as such under the Decisions in place at the time may be submitted to the CRO for retroactive recognition. The CRO will retroactively include these Peals in its analyses and records if it determines, based on good faith assessments of the available information about these Peals, that the requirements for a Peal under this document were met.

If such retroactively recognized Peals were not previously published as Peals in The Ringing World, the CRO will forward the details of these Peals to The Ringing World for publication, with the publication noting their retroactive recognition status.

If such retroactively recognized Peals were previously published as Peals in The Ringing World, the CRO will publish a notice in The Ringing World stating that these Peals have now been included in the CRO’s analyses and records, and providing the references to the original publications in The Ringing World.

If a Peal was previously published in the Ringing World in a contrived manner that enabled it to be recognized as a Peal under the Decisions in place at the time, the band may resubmit the Peal to the Ringing World for republication in the form that the band originally intended. The republication will include a cross-reference to the original publication.

3.

The CRO adds any new Methods that were rung in retroactively recognized Peals to the CRO Method Library, if these Methods have not already been added as a result of other Performances.
