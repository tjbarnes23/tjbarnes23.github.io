11. Related Ringing World Roles

1.

The Ringing World publishes Performance Reports comprising at least 1250 Changes that it receives, categorizing them according to the Performance Lengths in Section H.
The Ringing World publishes Performance Reports comprising less than 1250 Changes that it receives when these Performances name a new Method(s).
The Ringing World publishes other Performance Reports that it receives at its discretion.
The Ringing World may abbreviate Performance Reports as commercial considerations necessitate.

2.

The Ringing World publishes notices of Record Length attempts at least 14 days prior to the attempt, providing the notice is received in sufficient time for such publication.

3.

On receiving a Performance Report of a claimed Record Length, The Ringing World waits for the CRO’s Technical and Taxonomy Workgroup’s determination of whether a new Record Length has been rung before publishing the Report.

4.

When publishing the Performance Report of a Record Length, The Ringing World includes the umpire(s)’ report.
